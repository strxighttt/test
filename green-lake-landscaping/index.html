What We Grow

Native Plant Gardens

Drought-tolerant native plantings that support local pollinators and thrive without excessive water.

Eco-Friendly Lawn Care

Organic lawn programs that build healthy soil and reduce the need for synthetic inputs.

Rain Garden Design

Beautiful rain gardens that capture runoff, filter water, and add visual interest to low areas.

Smart Irrigation

Weather-based irrigation systems that deliver the right amount of water at the right time.

Tree Planting

Species-appropriate tree selection and planting for shade, screening, and long-term canopy health.

Organic Weed Control

Targeted, chemical-free weed management that keeps your landscape healthy and safe.

Working With Nature, Not Against It

Green Lake Landscaping believes the healthiest landscapes are the ones that work with nature. We use sustainable practices, native plants, and smart water management to create outdoor spaces that are as resilient as they are beautiful.

Whether you're looking to replace a thirsty lawn with native groundcovers, install a rain garden, or redesign your entire property with eco-conscious principles, we bring deep knowledge and a gentle hand to every project.
