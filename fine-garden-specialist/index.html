About Us

Passionate About Beautiful Gardens

The Fine Garden Specialist brings over 15 years of experience to every project. Based in Silver Spring, MD, we treat every garden as a living work of art — combining horticultural knowledge with thoughtful design to create outdoor spaces that inspire.
