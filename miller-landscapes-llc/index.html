Caring for Silver Spring, One Yard at a Time

At Miller Landscapes, family is at the heart of everything we do. What started as a small weekend operation has grown into a trusted landscaping company built on reliability, integrity, and old-fashioned hard work.

We believe in showing up on time, doing the job right the first time, and treating our customers like neighbors — because they are. From routine lawn care to full property transformations, we bring the same care to every job, big or small.

Quality you can count on. From a family you can trust.

Our Services

Lawn Maintenance

Hedge & Shrub Trimming
