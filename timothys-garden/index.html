Our Story

Founded in 1989, Timothy's Garden Inc has been transforming outdoor spaces in Silver Spring and the greater Washington D.C. area for over three generations. What started as a small neighborhood gardening service has grown into a full-scale design and maintenance studio.

We believe every garden tells a story. Our team combines horticultural expertise with artistic vision to create landscapes that are sustainable, beautiful, and deeply personal.
