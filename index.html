About Us

Bringing Design-First Landscapes to Montgomery County

Tulip Valley Landscaping was founded on a simple belief: your outdoor space should be as thoughtfully designed as the inside of your home. Based in Silver Spring, we specialize in custom landscape design that balances beauty, functionality, and the unique character of your property.

From intimate garden beds to full property transformations, we partner with homeowners who care about quality — combining horticultural knowledge with a refined design eye. Every project starts with a conversation, a site walk, and a vision for what your yard could become.

We're proud to serve our local community with reliable, detail-driven execution. No two landscapes are alike, and neither are our designs.

— Founded in Silver Spring
