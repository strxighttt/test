About Us

Family-owned, rooted in Silver Spring

Villeda Landscaping has been serving Montgomery County with reliable, high-quality landscaping since 2010. What started as a small lawn-mowing operation has grown into a full-service company trusted by hundreds of homeowners and businesses.
